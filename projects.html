Automatic Shiny Hunter

Equipping a Nintendo DS with an Arduino and a photoresistor, alongside some servos, the program essentially autonomously control the game by measuring light from the screen, and having servos make decisions based on those readings.

Yonder Dynamics

A student robotics organization at UC San Diego formally established in Fall 2016. As our primary project, we design and build an autonomous Mars rover for the annual University Rover Challenge (URC), a prestigious international competition for college students.

DIY 3-Axis CNC Machine

Designed and constructed my very own 3-Axis CNC (Computer Numerical Control) Machine, capable of milling, drilling, and engraving a variety of materials. Through a limited budget, innovated a fully modular design that surpassed pre-built machines in both cut-volume as well as cost.

Biometric Authenticating Hand Sanitizer Dispenser

The project consists of three main parts. The first part is the wooden house designed on Onshape which meant to be for 3D printing. The second part is the lock system which includes the microcontroller Arduino Uno R3, a fingerprint sensor, and a servo motor. The third part is the automatic dispenser which includes an infrared sensor, a PNP transistor, and a water pump. Both second and third parts have the same power supply through the microcontroller.

Object Manipulation with Computer Vision in a Simulated Environment

Aimed to control two robot arms to pick up boxes and place them at the target bin efficiently in a simulated environment (SAPIEN.ucsd.edu) using information from multiple but fixed cameras in the environment. The challenge is that the size of the boxes, bin, the spades(end-effector) to pick up the boxes vary from scene to scene. This produces a challenge of writing a code that produces the maximum overall success rate(number of boxes placed in the bin correctly / total number of boxes) and the overall efficiency(number of boxes placed / minute).

Vista

Scalable systems for Machine Learning are largely siloed into dataflow systems for structured data and deep learning (DL) systems for unstructured data. This gap has left workloads that jointly analyze both forms of data with poor systems support, leading to both low system efficiency and grunt work. Vista is a new data system that resolves systems issues by elevating entire transfer learning workloads to a declarative level on top of Parallel Dataflow and DL systems. Vista automatically optimizes configuration and execution of this workload to reduce computational redundancy and improve workload reliability.

HPLC Column Packing pump

HPLC Packing pumps at this company were controlled by hand. A process where chemists would monitor pressure, fluid output volume, time and control pressure accordingly to preset procedures. The project was using an STM32 Microcontroller to read feedback from 2 analog + 1 i2c sensor to output to a pressure controller through a DAC with an Op Amp. The presenter designed the circuitry and programmed the microcontroller.
